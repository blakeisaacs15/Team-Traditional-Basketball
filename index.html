Team Tradition Basketball was founded in 2026 by Dawson Gore — a former college player and assistant coach with a proven track record building programs in Westfield through the Jungle Basketball Club.

"We're not just starting a team. We're building a tradition that players, families, and coaches will be proud of for years to come."

In our inaugural season, we compete on the Under Armour Rise Circuit — developing players who understand the game, respect the process, and carry themselves with character on and off the court.

Coaching Staff

Led By Someone
Who's Been There

Dawson Gore
Founder & Head Coach · Team Tradition Basketball

"I've been on both sides — as a player grinding for a roster spot, and as a coach helping others reach their potential. I started this program because I know what young players in this area are capable of."

Dawson Gore brings a rare combination of playing experience and coaching expertise to Team Tradition. Having competed at the collegiate level and coached on college staffs, he understands the game at every layer.

A product of Indiana basketball himself, Dawson already has proven roots in the Westfield community through the Jungle Basketball Club — which he founded and continues to run alongside Team Tradition.

What We Stand For

Built On Three Pillars

01
Development First
Every rep, every practice, every game is about making the player better. We prioritize growth over wins — because developed players win more.
02
Character Matters
We expect effort, accountability, and respect from our players and coaches. The habits formed here follow you everywhere.
03
Compete Hard
We show up ready to compete at every level. When you wear Tradition, you represent something bigger than yourself.
